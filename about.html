About Our Website

Your solution for profanity detection, subtitle generation, and audio extraction.

Overview

👉Our website is designed to make video content safe, accessible, and user-friendly. The tool focuses on detecting profane words in video audio, generating accurate subtitles, and providing the option to extract audio from videos.

Key Features

  • Profanity Detection: Advanced AI algorithms identify and flag bad words in video audio, helping to maintain content safety for all audiences.
  • Subtitle Generation: Automatically creates precise subtitles for videos, enhancing accessibility for the hearing impaired and non-native speakers.
  • Audio Download: Allows users to extract and download audio from videos for various purposes, such as transcription or analysis.
  • Customizable Filters: Users can adjust detection filters based on cultural or regional preferences.
  • Detailed Reports: Generates reports highlighting flagged words, their timestamps, and associated video content for review.

Why Choose Our System?

👉This system is ideal for content creators, educators, parents, and businesses who want to ensure their video content is safe, professional, and suitable for all audiences. It provides a streamlined approach to video analysis and processing, saving time and effort while searching for unethical content.
